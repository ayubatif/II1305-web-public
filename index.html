What is Upay?

Upay is a revolutionary new approach to bill splitting. While traditional bill splitting apps will only allow their users to calculate how much each person owes and keep track of an event, Upay allows the users to pay each other back immediately with integrated payment solutions. Upay can be used both in Sweden and around the world with multiple currency options to end the constant conversions needed while travelling. Finally, Upay brings advanced technological solutions to make adding payments as seamless as possible such as receipt scanning and a full history of bills and payments.
