Landing Screen

The Sp/it app

Sp/it is a free Android and iOS application to help you split expenses within groups and keep track of your finances. It helps you divide up the cost from that group dinner or weekend without the mess of calculating who owes what to whom. To settle a payment, Sp/it is fully integrated with Swish to make your payment experience as smooth as possible.

How Sp/it works

In the app, you can choose to log in with an account or continue as a guest user. You can create events with various friends and divide your costs. Expenses can be split equally across event members or you can choose a custom allocation. To give you a smooth app using experience, our algorithm is designed to always keep the number of transactions at a minimum. Leaving the party early – settle up your debt while still allowing your friends to continue adding new expenses to the event. Lost track of your finances – just have a look at your activity page which summarizes all your ongoing events and gives you a detailed overview on your outstanding debt or how much you can expect to get back. You can also add friends to your contacts list and change your profile picture.

TEAM

Who are we?

Sp/it was developed during a project course at the Royal Institute of Technology(KTH) by seven students from different programs at campus Kista.

How Sp/it was developed

We used Scrum as the framework for the development. The functionalities of Sp/it were divided into smaller tasks, letting the team focus on key features with the possibilities to make changes when unpredictable challenges occurred. Every morning we had a meeting discussing problems both about the app and as a group, making the productivity increase each day. We developed Sp/it using Flutter with the programming language Dart. To handle authentication and real-time database services we used Firebase.
